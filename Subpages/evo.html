EVO

EVO is a game prototype that targets on mobile platform.

It is a turn-based strategy mobile game with multiple handcraft levels. The player controls units like chess pieces on a grid-based chessboard with the goal of defeating all enemies.

Each grid in the chessboard terrain corresponds to an action that the unit can perform. The grid is randomised at the beginning of the battle and after the grid is consumed. Players will be able to build their own team, level up their units along the game to prepare for levels of higher difficulties.
