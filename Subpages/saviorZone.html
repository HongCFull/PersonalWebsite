Link to App Store

"Savior Zone," as a key milestone in my career. Serving as the first commercially released game, "Savior Zone" showcases my expertise in the field of game development. I took on the responsibility of developing the entire gameplay system, ensuring smooth and engaging player experiences. Additionally, I undertook game optimization efforts to enhance performance and deliver seamless gameplay. Alongside gameplay mechanics, I incorporated various facets, including Unity animations, audio effects, and user interface design, to create a comprehensive and immersive gaming experience. "Savior Zone" represents my passion for game development and demonstrates my ability to tackle diverse aspects of game production.
